UX Case Study: Porrada

Empowering Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) Practitioners at Every Level

Project Overview

  • Timeline: May 2025 — Present
  • Author: Sam Sexton

Porrada is a web application created to support Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) athletes - from white belts to black belts - by offering a personalized digital training partner. With features like technique tracking, training logs, instructional libraries, and community-based feedback, Porrada aims to improve progression, structure learning, and foster a strong sense of community among practitioners.
